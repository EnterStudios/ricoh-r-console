Connection Procedure

① Connect your RICOH R to a HDMI monitor and a PC/Mac.

You cannot use the RICOH R Console when outputting with USB Video Class.

(PC/Mac) RICOH R Console
A monitor with an HDMI port
Connect the cable to the micro USB port (for power) at the bottom of the device

② Check that the LED on the side of your RICOH R is solid blue or red.

If the LED is solid green, press the Shooting Mode button on the side of your RICOH R to change the mode.

LED indicator
Switch mode button
Green: USB Video Class (UVC) out
Blue: HDMI out
Red: Recording to the microSD card

③ On the drop-down list at the top of this screen, select the RICOH R that you connected.
